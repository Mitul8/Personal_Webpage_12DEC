About Me

Glad you are here. Hey everyone! I'm Deepak. I write and document for individuals who may benefit from the learnings and experiences I had. The content came from many different dimensions of life, ranging from purely technical to utterly artistic. This became my inspiration page to give back whatever little things I could collect in my journey. As I have gotten up in years and developed views on a variety of things, I've come to realize that I can't just keep these thoughts to myself if they're going to disappear - the least I can do is shine a spotlight on them. Sometimes a small paragraph will do; sometimes, even a few pages won't be enough. In short, it is about the ideas that symbolize personal reflection. I hope some of you may find them helpful in your own way. A little caveat about content style, it is bit terse!
